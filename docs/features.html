OT features menu

  • Superiors and inferiors

    Upgrade includes superior and inferior figure sets. These can combine with lowercase characters to make sophisticated typographic compositions.

  • Fractions

    Using the [frac] feature makes 1/2 show as ½.

  • Small caps

    The [smcp] feature turns lowercase characters into small caps. The [c2sc] feature turns capitals into small caps. If both [smcp] and [s2sc] are enabled, then lowercase AND capitals become small caps.

  • Types of figures

    The [tnum] feature makes figures and currency symbols show on tabular widths of 650/1000EM. The [onum] and [smcp] features create lowercase (oldstyle) figures and figures at small-cap height, respectively.

  • Slashed zeros

    When the [zero] feature set is enabled, zeros get a slash to clearly differentiate them from Os.
