OT features menu

  • Superiors and inferiors

    Figures and lower case make many typographic combinations.

  • Fractions

    Using the [frac] feature, makes 1/2 show as ½.

  • Small caps

    The [smcp] features makes lower case show as small caps. The [c2sc] features make the capitals show as small caps. Or both.

  • Types of figures

    The [tnum] feature makes figures and currency symbols show on tabular widths of 650/1000EM. The [onum] and [smcp] features make respectively lower case (old style) figures and figures on small cap height.

  • Slashed zeros

    With the [zero] feature set, zeros get a slash to make clear visual separation with Oâ€™s.
